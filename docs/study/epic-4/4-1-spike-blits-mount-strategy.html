Mental model (≤30s)

Lab W teaches buffers → textures → shaders → draw calls by hand. Home uses Blits on Lightning 3: same GPU-backed class of problems, scene-graph DX.

Interview line: “I practiced raw WebGL vocabulary in Lab W, then embedded Blits in-page under a thin DOM Shell with quit-on-leave — not production Lightning years.”

Why in-page (not iframe)

OptionOutcome in 4.1
In-page embed Shipped — one Vite process; Shell Back stays simple; Launch into stage HTMLElement
iframe Rejected — second app/server + postMessage for Back/D-pad

Gotcha we hit: Blits templates with w="100%" need a ready parent.node. Hello-world uses absolute sizes (800×450 rects) until the rail story owns layout.
